## 1. The skew product, formalized  `[PROVEN]`

**State space / base / cocycle.** Let `(b_n) вҲҲ {0,1}^в„•` be the parity sequence (`b_n = c_n mod 2`, exogenous
to this section: it is the orbit's own itinerary). The base map is the one-sided shift `T` on `{0,1}^в„•`.
The carry `S_n` is an integer-valued **additive cocycle over `T`**, generated by a Г—3 base action with an
additive injection at the moving seam:

> `[PROVEN]` `S_{n+1} = 3 S_n + 2^n b_n`, `S_0 = 0`; closed form
> `S_n = ОЈ_{j<n} 3^{n-1-j} 2^j b_j` (0 failures, big-int, nвҲҲ{10,вҖҰ,99999}); seam identity
> `2^n c_n + S_n = 8В·3^n` and `S_n вүЎ 8В·3^n (mod 2^n)` (0 failures).

So `S_n` is a **Г—3-driven (skew) cocycle**: each step applies the Г—3 в„ӨвӮӮ-isometry (carry-mixing, the *same*
operator whose orbit `8В·3^n` is the first diagonal) and **adds** `2^n b_n`. The read

> `Пғ_n = bit_{n+k}(S_n)`, the **second diagonal**,

is the position-`(n+k)` digit of the cocycle, the index advancing with the clock вҖ” a *moving-diagonal* read,
exactly as the first diagonal `d_n = bit_{n+k}(8В·3^n)` (`ODD_3ADIC_ODOMETER В§1`).

**Where the injection lands vs. where the read happens (the geometry).** `S_n вүЎ 8В·3^n (mod 2^n)` means the
low `n` bits of `S_n` are frozen (= those of `8В·3^n`); bit `n` is the **seam** between this tame block and the
free zone. The injection `2^n b_n` enters at the seam `n` (it becomes the new seam: `S_{n+1} вүЎ 8В·3^{n+1}
mod 2^{n+1}`). The read sits at `n+k`, **`k` positions into the free zone above the seam.** Crucially
`S_n` depends only on `b_0..b_{n-1}`; **`b_n` is NOT in `S_n`** (verified: flipping `b_n` leaves `S_n`
unchanged). The injection is therefore *below* and *one step behind* the read.
